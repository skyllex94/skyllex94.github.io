Richness in Content

The History of our service dates back to 1993. We originally started with a couple of old recording from boxing championships from the late 50's. After lending the collections for a couple of dollars, we decided we wanted to expand our vision and create a boxing enviromnent for the fans of the sport.

In the years to come, the scope of influence dramatically grew and from a lending company, we started introducing boxing supplies. Things started to turn faster by 2002, we were already branching out of the lending and retail business and getting in the service vanue with proffessional training. Both services have been growing together ever since.
